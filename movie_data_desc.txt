The file includes the 100 movies from US. It shows the rating of
the movies and the factor that may affect the rating. The variable 
explanations are given below.


genre: film's genre

gross: gross of the movie (in USD)

cost: cost of the movie (in USD)

no_of_viewer: the number of people who watch the movie

year : the year where the movie was released 

duration: the duration of the movie (in minutes)

outside: the place in the movie is outside of US (yes, no)

netflix: the movie was streamed on netflix (yes,no)

rating: the rating of the movie(1-10)
